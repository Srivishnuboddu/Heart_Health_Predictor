About Us

Welcome to the Heart Health Predictor! Our goal is to provide users with an easy-to-use tool for assessing heart health risks based on common health indicators. This project is dedicated to raising awareness about heart health and promoting a healthy lifestyle.

Tips for Good Heart Health

  • Maintain a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
  • Engage in regular physical activities like walking, jogging, or swimming.
  • Avoid smoking and limit alcohol consumption.
  • Manage stress through relaxation techniques such as meditation or yoga.
  • Keep a check on blood pressure and cholesterol levels regularly.
  • Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep per night to support overall heart health.
  • Drink plenty of water throughout the day to help maintain overall health.
  • Avoid tobacco use and exposure to secondhand smoke, as it significantly increases heart disease risk.
